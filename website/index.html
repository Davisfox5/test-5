How It Works

From Call to Insight in
Under 60 Seconds

A seamless pipeline that captures, transcribes, analyzes, and delivers — automatically.

01

Capture

Connect the phone system you already run — Twilio, Telnyx, SignalWire, Zoom Phone, Webex, or RingCentral — or simply upload recordings. Audio is transcribed and then discarded, never stored long-term.

02

Transcribe

Every word is captured with 98.5% accuracy, live or from a recording — and Linda automatically labels who said what, so you always know which side of the call it came from.

03

Analyze

Claude AI extracts summaries, sentiment, action items, coaching insights, and drafts follow-up emails automatically.

04

Deliver

Push insights to Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, or email. Your team gets what they need, where they already work.
