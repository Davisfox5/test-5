How It Works

From Call to Insight in
Under 60 Seconds

A seamless pipeline that captures, transcribes, analyzes, and delivers — automatically.

01

Capture

Connect your phone system through Twilio, SignalWire, or Telnyx Media Streams, or upload recordings directly. Audio is transcribed and discarded — never stored long-term.

02

Transcribe

Deepgram Nova-3 for live and batch ASR, with self-hosted faster-whisper and pyannote.audio diarization as a fallback. Speaker diarization labels who said what.

03

Analyze

Claude AI extracts summaries, sentiment, action items, coaching insights, and drafts follow-up emails automatically.

04

Deliver

Push insights to Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, or email. Your team gets what they need, where they already work.
