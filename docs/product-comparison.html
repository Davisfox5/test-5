AI Profile Where AI sits in the product On top of a compliance archive — the archive is still the product; AI is the new value layer being built out (AI Agent today, AI Orchestrator on roadmap) Inside the QA / analytics workflow — the workflow is the product, AI does the scoring, summarizing, and trend detection Two AI add-ons sitting on top of the phone service — SI for analytics, SimplyAI for summaries; the phone service itself is still the core product AI is the product, exposed through APIs; the UI is intentionally thin
Real-time vs after-the-call Mostly after-the-call analytics today; agentic / autonomous workflows are roadmap Both — live agent assist + live listen + post-call coaching Mostly post-call; SI fires real-time alerts on missed opportunities, but no live agent coaching Both — live coaching over WebSocket and post-call extraction are both core design goals

Position Biggest strength Trusted compliance archive across 100+ regulations; multi-tenant from day one; channel-led white-label; published, accessible entry pricing for the Teams use case Polished end-to-end AI-QA workflow; broadest AI feature set in the mid-market today; IMetrics & Ask AI lead the “survey-less CX” story Bundled with the phone service — simplest path for SMBs who want one vendor; two-product split lets buyers start light (SimplyAI email digests) and graduate to deeper analytics (SI) API-first, white-label-ready, modern multi-LLM architecture; full lifecycle (live + post-call) on a single stack
Biggest weakness Portal-first — you live in their UI; AI was retrofitted onto a recording archive, not built-in; agentic AI still on roadmap Centered on contact-center QA; not white-labeled; no API-first / headless mode; thin fit for sales / CS teams that aren’t shaped like a contact center Locked to the SimpleVoIP voice service; shallow AI depth (no QA scorecards, no live coaching, no churn modeling); no APIs, no white-label, no language breadth advertised No turnkey supervisor UI; not a compliance archive; thinner prompt-playground UX than MiaRec; no audio-level PII redaction yet
Best-fit buyer Regulated business that needs an immutable archive first and AI second — or a Microsoft Teams shop that wants compliant recording with analytics for ~$15/user/month Mid-market contact center wanting turnkey AI-driven QA + survey-less CX out of the box SMB that wants phone service and a light AI layer from a single vendor — SimplyAI for the email-summary buyer, SI for the analytics-curious owner-operator MSP, CPaaS, vertical SaaS, or product team that wants to embed conversation intelligence under their own brand — or any sales / CS org that needs deeper AI than QA tools provide
